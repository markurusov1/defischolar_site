# Welcome to DeFi Scholar!

## The What and Why of DeFi

>- DeFi is short for “DeCentralized Finance” 
>- For aspiring finance majors, studying DeFi is essential because it represents the programmable future of money, 
   > thereby equipping students to thrive in an industry where traditional barriers are crumbling in favor of transparent, efficient systems
>- "If you're not looking at DeFi, you're not going to make it in finance"

## Approach 
>- “Learn together” – this guide is by high schoolers for high schoolers 
>- Keeping things simple and using examples 
>- Explain basics and leave deep dives for projects or future study
